=== Rules ===
== Setup Phase ==
1. You must place all of your ships on the board
2. No two ships may overlap
== Playing Phase ==
1. Pick any of your opponent's squares to shoot. If you hit an opponent's ship, you may shoot again. If you miss, it is the other player's turn.
2. If you sink all of your opponent's ships, you win. If they sink all of your ships first, they win.
